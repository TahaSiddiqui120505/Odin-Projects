Biryani

This biryani recipe takes a little work

but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, heat the ghee over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until softened, about 5-7 minutes.
  2. Add the cumin, cinnamon, and peppercorns, and cook for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
