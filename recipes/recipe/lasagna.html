Lasagna

This lasagna recipe takes a little work

but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic
  • Tomato products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter), or olive oil

Steps

  2. 2 cups (280 ml) heavy cream
