Pizza

This pizza recipe takes a little work

but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 pinch white sugar
  • 3 â…“ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the warm water, yeast, and sugar.
  2. Stir in the flour until well combined.
  3. Add the olive oil and mix until well blended.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 10 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover with plastic wrap.
  6. Let the dough rise for 40-60 minutes, or until doubled in size.
  7. Divide the dough into 8 equal parts. Place each part on a floured surface and let it rise for an additional 20-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the middle is softened.
